Street Dog Passion

I have many passions, to be honest. But none, absolutely none beats my passion for dogs. And specifically: shelter dogs. Those thankful furry souls that know what it is to have to shelter from the cold and rain, from the snow and thunder, and yes, from the sticks and stones, kicks and whips many villagers in some countries treat them to.
Together with my husband, we have adopted quite a bunch of them and they enlighten our home with joy. We strongly advice people to adopt not only one dog more more than one, and kindly suggest to take as many tiny ones as your sofa can hold because the tiny ones are very easy to take care of, and, they are the ones that suffer the most on the streets.
